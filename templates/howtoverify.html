How to verify the balance via Google Pay

Congratulation, you are just one step behind getting this loan!

Verification Process Terms And Condition

Before we deposit the loan in your account, you need to verify that you have a good financial status to receive this loan.
We do not check long time bank or credit history to provide the loan.
However, you need to verify that at this time you are capable of repaying your loan payment on time.
So for that, you need to show the balance of $100 to prove you can easily repay this loan.

Please note: We are not going to take the $100, you just have to verify it via Google Pay and then we will deposit the loan via EFT (Electronic funds transfer) and you will receive it within 45 minutes in your bank account.



Why can't you just verify it from my bank account?

We are not allowed to verify the balance via the bank directly and we do not accept Screenshot and Bank statement for security reasons.

Why only Google Pay?

You can verify the balance instantly via the Google Pay and it's secure.
You can verify the balance online, no need to go anywhere.

$100 will remain in Google Pay and can be transferred easily back to your bank account without any fees or hassle after the verification.

Steps to verify the balance via Google Pay.

1. Open Playstore in your phone and search for GOOGLE PAY to install or update the app .

2. Open the Google Pay app.
3. Select your account (email) and click on Continue.
4. Enter your phone number to verify.
5. Tap on next and keep pressing yes to proceed.
6. Click on profile. Located at top right corner with $0 written on it.
Click on Google Pay balance and then click on Verify my identity. Write your information and click on next to verify.
After that, you will redirect to add debit card option. Please add it.
7. Click on profile icon again and click on Google Pay balance and then press Add Money. Click Next and select your debit card to add the balance.
Please do not select to add with bank account because that will take 3-5 days. With debit card, it's immediately.
8. Once done, you will see the $100 on Google pay balance.
9. Go back to main Google Pay screen and press on "Send or Money Request". Search for our email address provided by Live Chat support.
10. Send the payment. That's it. After this process, we will refund the $100 immediately back to your account with the loan within 45 minutes as our terms and condition.

How much time does it take to receive the loan after the verification process?

Only 45 minutes.

When will I receive the loan documents?

We send loan documents to a physical address, but you can request a copy of the loan document to your email address.
