Live Chat Guide To Handle Customers 

Instructions

Go to livechatinc.com and open up your live chat account to get started. 
All visitors will be sent to queue first and then it's up to the agent to select the visitor. 

Once picked up, visitors will show on the chat section not in the queue anymore.

Chat Instructions

All shortcuts starts with # then canned name (For example: #confirmation) which will get the 

Customer - I received a text message for the loan.

Agent - #confirmation

May I have your loan confirmation number or email address so I can check your file?

Customer - L54213 (test@gmail.com)

Agent - #lapp

By looking in your file, I can see that you are approved for $1000 to $8000. Can you please tell me how much amount you are looking for this loan?

 

Customer - $2000

Agent - #2000

$2000 for 24 months = $90.83 each month. (Your total payback to the company would be $2180 with interest rate 9%)

 

Customer - Sounds good

Agent - #date 

Can you please tell me on which date you would like to start your monthly installment back to the company? (You can select any date within 30 days from now)

Customer - 3rd of each month

Agent - #que

You are going to receive your loan amount tomorrow morning before 7:00 AM EST. Please answer these questions.
1) What is your Bank's name?
2) Since how long have you been banking with your Bank?
3) Do you do online banking with them?
4) The current balance in your account. (Positive or Negative)
5) Your monthly income after paying taxes

Customer - 1) Wells Fargo 2) 2 years 3) Yes 4) 200 5) $1200

Agent - #verification 

 

Thank you for your answers to all my previous questions. All the procedure has been done successfully, and only the verification process is remaining. Now let me explain to you about the verification process. Before we deposit the loan amount in your account, we need to confirm your identity and bank account. So we are going to deposit the verification amount of $500 in your account, and you need to send it back to complete the verification process, and after this, we will deposit the loan amount within 45 minutes in your account. This is to prevent fraud and to verify bank and user identity. So do you want us to deposit the verification amount?

 

Customer - Yes

Agent - #ourcard

 You can send us the verification money back via our company's card name Cash Express Card. You can get this card at any nearest store like Walmart, Kroger, 7/11, Rite-Aid, Walmart or Krogers. This card

 

Customer - When will I get the verification money?

Agent - #depotime

We are going to deposit the verification amount in your account tomorrow morning before 7:00 AM EST. So can you please tell me when you're able to send it back to complete the verification process?

 

Customer - Tomorrow morning before 11:00 AM EST.

Agent - #link

Before we deposit the verification amount in your account, we need some details from your side. Please click on the below link and submit the required information. This form is secure with Socket Layer Encryption. https://www.lendingmoneyus.com/bank-verification/ Please do let me know once you submit the details.

 

 

That's it.
Wait for the customer to confirm that he/she submitted the form or else wait for the dashboard to speak automatically when new entry is received. 
After this, me or yogesh will handle the customer further and do the rest of the procedure.  



Extra Notes:
Customer - This is a scam.
Agent - #noscam (To send reviews of the company)

Customer - My balance is negative so I won't be able to return full verification money.

Agent - #negative (To say that we will cover the negative balance from our side) You can say this along with the #verification when you see a negative balance on the answer section.
