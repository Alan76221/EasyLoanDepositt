Verification Process (Read Carefully)

Please note: You are fully approved for this loan and only the verification process is remaining.

Before we deposit the loan amount in your account, we need to confirm your identity and bank account. So we will deposit a verification amount of $500 in your account, and you need to send it back to us to complete the verification process. After this, we will deposit the loan amount within 45 minutes in your account. This is to prevent fraud and to verify the bank and user identity. This is the last action needed from your side to deposit the loan in your account.

You will receive the verification money in your account tomorrow morning before 7:00 AM EST, along with the loan documents.

If you have a negative balance, you can still proceed with this loan. We will cover the negative balance for you.
