Loan Deposit Options

Congratulations on being eligible to receive a loan through our lending services. We understand that you may have urgent financial needs, and we are here to assist you in the most efficient manner possible. To ensure a smooth and expedited process, we offer you two options for receiving your loan:

Option 1: Bank Transfer This method typically takes a minimum of 10 to 15 days to complete. We will automatically verify your bank information and deposit the loan using the traditional bank deposit method.

Option 2: EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) With this option, we can deposit the loan directly into your bank account, and it usually takes only 45 minutes, with some transactions completing in just a few minutes. However, there is a one-time verification process for EFT, requiring a $50 verification fee. Which will be refunded to you. To verify, you need to upload $50 to your cash app and send it to our company. We will verify the balance and deposit the loan and refund of $50 within 45 minutes. To receive the loan deposit, you will need to verify $50 via Cash App. please contact our live chat support. We are here to assist you every step of the way.
